# Business Goals

The application should replace manual Excel-based offer pricing with a controlled web workflow.

Goals:

- Reduce human error in offer calculations.
- Preserve offer history and calculation snapshots.
- Allow each project to have independent material prices.
- Produce fast pricing outputs grouped by scope, line, and part.
- Make MTO and material data reusable across offers.
- Keep base data for materials, scopes, parts, and MTO rows out of hard-coded application logic.
- Make final prices traceable back to MTO rows and project material prices.
- Prepare the system for future CU scopes and new MTO versions.
